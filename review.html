Historical 2023 3h

Oppenheimer

★★★★★

Score: 9.5 / 10

"A breathtaking historical epic that redefines cinema."

Our Review

Christopher Nolan's Oppenheimer is a cinematic achievement unlike anything in recent memory. The film tells the story of J. Robert Oppenheimer — the physicist who led the Manhattan Project — with a bold non-linear structure and jaw-dropping practical filmmaking.

Cillian Murphy delivers a career-best performance, carrying every frame with quiet intensity. The supporting cast, including Robert Downey Jr. and Emily Blunt, add tremendous depth to every scene.

The IMAX sequences are stunning. The Trinity test sequence, filmed practically, is one of the greatest moments in modern cinema.

Verdict

A must-watch. A rare film that is both a blockbuster spectacle and a thoughtful meditation on genius, guilt, and the weight of history. One of the best films of the decade.
